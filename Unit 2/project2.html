Carryout Job

The job of the worker in carryout is to answer the phones and take care of as many questions as possible. Along with answering phones comes the respnsibility of taking orders, placing them, and making sure to get all sides that come with the order.

Carryout employees are required to make all salads orders, fill all drink orders, fill all pie orders, and to get all soups and salad items for the customer (among other resposibilities).
