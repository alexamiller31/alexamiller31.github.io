Cody Johnson

Cody Johnson was born in East Texas where he was raised by his parents Sheila and Carl Johnson. Cody began playing music when he was just 12 years old and is now a charted country music star.

Cody has self-released 6 albums including his number two ranked album on the Billboards Country Album Chart, Gotta be me. In October of 2021 Cody released his first major label album, Human:The Double Album.
